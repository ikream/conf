2nd International Conference on MISI

The 2nd International Conference on Mathematics and Intelligent Systems in Industry (MISI 2025) will be held on July 10-11, 2025, at the National School of Applied Sciences (ENSA), Safi, Morocco.

MISI 2025 aims to bring together researchers, engineers, academics, and industry professionals to exchange and share their experiences and research results on all aspects of Applied Mathematics and Intelligent Systems for Industry 4.0. The conference provides an interdisciplinary platform to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and challenges in these fields.

Conference Topics

  • Mathematical Methods for Industrial Applications
  • Optimization and Control Systems
  • Modeling and Simulation in Industry
  • Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Systems
  • Smart Manufacturing and Industrial Automation
  • Digital Twins and Predictive Maintenance
  • Data Analytics, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning
  • Computer Vision in Industrial Applications
  • IoT and Edge Computing for Industry
  • Sustainable and Ethical Industry Transformation

Target Audience

  • Researchers and PhD students
  • Industry professionals and engineers
  • Professors and academic experts
  • Master’s students and young innovators
  • Startups and developers of smart industrial solutions

Paper Submission

Papers can be submitted in English or French. Accepted papers will be published in the official conference proceedings with ISBN. Selected high-quality papers will be invited for extended versions in indexed journals.

Organizers & Partners

The conference is organized by the Moroccan Association of Applied Mathematics and Intelligent Systems (AM-MISI), in partnership with Cadi Ayyad University, ENSA Safi, and other academic and industrial collaborators.
